small side table

a wood and metal side table. a piece that's both functional and visually appealing.

design process

this table was crafted to showcase the natural beauty of wood while incorporating clean metal elements for structural integrity and visual contrast. i hand-turned the table top on a lathe to create a smooth finish with a bordering lip, and welded the metal frame to create a durable and sturdy structure.

materials

  • solid wood top
  • welded metal frame
  • protective wood finish
  • clear-coated metal components

features

  • durable construction
  • modern aesthetic
  • perfect height for side table use
  • contrasting material elements
