kitchen cart

a mobile kitchen cart combining industrial metal construction with a warm butcher block top, designed to provide both functionality and style in modern kitchens.

design process

the cart was designed to maximize utility while maintaining a clean, industrial aesthetic. the combination of metal and wood creates a perfect balance between durability and warmth.

materials

  • steel frame construction
  • solid wood butcher block top
  • heavy-duty caster wheels
  • powder-coated metal finish

features

  • mobile design with locking wheels
  • spacious butcher block work surface
  • industrial-grade construction
  • modern industrial aesthetic
