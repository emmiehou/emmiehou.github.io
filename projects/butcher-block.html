butcher block

handcrafted butcher block cutting board made from arranged strips of hardwood, including poplar, walnut, cherry, maple, and redwood. this cutting board showcases natural wood tones and provides durable functionality ideal for kitchen use.

design process

this was created through a multi-stage process:

  • planned detailed measurements and selected complementary hardwoods to highlight color variations and grain patterns
  • cut and prepared individual hardwood strips, arranging them strategically for wood contrast
  • glued the strips together
  • surfaced the board using planing and sanding techniques to achieve a smooth, flat, and even finish
  • chamfered the edges to enhance aesthetics
  • finished with food-safe oil, enhancing the wood and protecting against moisture

materials

  • poplar, walnut, cherry, maple, and redwood
  • food-safe oil

features

  • durable hardwood
  • varied and aesthetically pleasing wood combinations
  • chamfered edges
  • food-safe oil finish
