Improved RuleDuino

June 2025

Reverse engineered and improved the "RuleDuino." This device is used widely in our lab to record live data and laser stimulate target regions of the brain during behavioral experiments. After figuring out the schematic, I designed the most optimal layout within the constraints of the cardboard box being the vessel, and created a "RuleDuino" with a more stable structure with a cleaner appearance.
