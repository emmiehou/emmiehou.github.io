milk speaker

a unique bluetooth speaker housed in a repurposed milk bottle, combining modern technology with nostalgic design.

design process

the speaker was designed to preserve the iconic shape of the milk bottle while integrating modern audio components. careful consideration was given to acoustics and component placement.

materials

  • vintage milk bottle
  • bluetooth audio components
  • custom mounting hardware
  • integrated led lighting

features

  • wireless bluetooth connectivity
  • ambient lighting effects
  • vintage aesthetic
  • quality audio output
