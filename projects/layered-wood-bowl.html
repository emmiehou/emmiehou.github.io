layered wood bowl

a decorative bowl crafted from multiple layers of wood, showcasing the natural beauty of different wood grains in a harmonious design.

design process

each layer was carefully selected and arranged to create an interesting pattern of wood grains. the bowl was shaped on a lathe to achieve its final form, emphasizing the layered construction.

materials

  • various hardwood species
  • food-safe finish
  • wood adhesive
  • protective coating

features

  • unique grain patterns
  • smooth finish
  • decorative display piece
  • durable construction
