milk speaker

a unique bluetooth speaker housed in a wooden "milk carton", combining my loves for design and drinking milk :)

design process

the speaker was designed to preserve the iconic shape of the milk bottle while integrating modern audio components. careful consideration was given to acoustics and component placement.

materials

  • wooden milk carton
  • bluetooth audio components
  • custom laser-cut acrylic and wooden hardware
  • integrated led lighting and speaker
  • custom PCB and circuitry

features

  • wireless bluetooth connectivity
  • sound-sensitive LED lighting
  • modern aesthetic
  • high-quality audio output
