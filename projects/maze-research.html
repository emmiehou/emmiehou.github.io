maze research

This summer, I developed a novel behavioral assay to test cognitive flexibility in mouse models. with the amazing mentorship of Christine Liu and all those in the Sohal Lab at UCSF, I designed, built, and ran experiments on a modular labrything maze to investigate how mice adapt to changing environments.

research focus

I joined the Sohal Lab at UCSF in spring of 2025 to study cognitive flexibility involving the mPFC neural circuits in mice. This summer, I participated in the UCSF Undergraduate Computational Neuroscience program, where I attended workshops on professional development, scientific research skills, and conducted my own research.
